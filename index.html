About Me

Hello, my name is Ayumi, though many people also know me as Ayaka. I am a 27-year-old digital artist, VTuber, and creator behind Ayakami Studio.

My dream is to create artwork, characters, and content that can bring comfort to others. Through my art and creative projects, I hope to spread kindness, warmth, and love to people who may feel alone or misunderstood.

Japanese aesthetics, cherry blossoms, quiet nights, and soft emotions inspire much of my work. I believe creativity can become a safe place where people can relax, connect, and simply feel at home.

Thank you for visiting my little corner of the world.
