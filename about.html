✿ Who I Am

Hello, my name is Ayumi, though many people also know me as Ayaka. I am a 27-year-old digital artist, VTuber, and creator behind Ayakami Studio.

✿ My Dreams

My dream is to create artwork, characters, and content that bring comfort, kindness, and warmth to others.

✿ My Inspirations

Cherry blossoms, quiet nights, Japanese aesthetics, emotional storytelling, and cozy spaces inspire my work.

✿ A Message From Me

If my art or content can help even one person feel less alone, then I feel I have succeeded.
