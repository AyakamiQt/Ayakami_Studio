About Me

Ayumi

✿ Who I Am

Hello, my name is Ayumi, though many people also know me as Ayaka. I am a 27-year-old digital artist, VTuber, and creator behind Ayakami Studio.

✿ My Dreams

My dream is to create artwork and content that brings comfort, kindness, and warmth to others, especially people who feel alone.

✿ My Inspirations

Cherry blossoms, Japanese aesthetics, quiet nights, soft emotions, virtual worlds, and cozy spaces inspire much of my work.
