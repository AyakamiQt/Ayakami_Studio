About Me

Ayumi | Ayaka

✿ Who I Am

Hello, my name is Ayumi, though many people also know me as Ayaka. I am a 27-year-old digital artist, VTuber, and creator behind Ayakami Studio.

✿ My Dreams

My dream is to create artwork, characters, and content that can bring comfort to others. I hope to spread kindness, warmth, and love through my creations.

✿ My Inspirations

Japanese aesthetics, cherry blossoms, quiet nights, soft colors, virtual worlds, and emotional storytelling inspire much of my work.

✿ A Message From Me

If my art or content can make even one person feel less alone, then I feel I have succeeded. Thank you for visiting my little corner of the world.
